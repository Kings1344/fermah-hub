What Fermah is Building: Solving the ZK Bottleneck

Fermah is building a decentralized, two-sided marketplace designed to solve the complexity and high cost associated with generating Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs). While ZKPs are essential for blockchain scalability and privacy, the current ecosystem faces major challenges:

  • High Hardware Cost: Proof generation requires massive computational power, mainly from expensive GPUs and ASICs.
  • Orchestration Complexity: Projects must manage complex infrastructure for proving jobs, creating centralization risks.
  • Inconsistent Speed: Latency varies widely based on resource availability and proof complexity.

Fermah’s mission is to make ZKPs cheap, fast, and reliably accessible for any developer. It ensures credible neutrality by supporting all major proof systems — zkVMs, zkEVMs, Groth16, PLONK, and STARKs.
